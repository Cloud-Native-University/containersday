Erik Taveras is a Backend Engineer with over 10 years of experience, specialized in automation and Artificial Intelligence for companies. Through Taveras Solutions LLC, he transforms manual operational tasks into automated workflows, focusing on custom solutions and self-hosted architectures. Erik has built production AI systems, including 24/7 sales agents for WhatsApp and complex automations with n8n, Python, and Django.

In addition to his consulting work, he is a passionate educator and creator of the AutoDev community. He has trained more than 1,600 developers and led the deployment of over 150 systems that, combined, have processed more than $2 million in revenue.

### Key Achievements

* **+10 Years of Experience**: Specialization in systems architecture, microservices, and REST APIs.
* **+150 Production Systems**: Stably deployed for various clients and market niches.
* **+1,600 Trained Developers**: Active training in automation technologies and backend web development.
* **Self-Hosted Architecture**: Solid focus on deployments using Docker and open-source tools like n8n, granting full infrastructure control to the client.

### Relevant Use Cases (AI & Automation Portfolio)

* **Autonomous Sales Agents**: Development of an AI-driven sales agent for WhatsApp, capable of qualifying leads, handling objections, and closing sales 24/7 using advanced models and a multi-tenant architecture.
* **Custom ERP Systems (Django + PostgreSQL + AWS)**: Construction of robust backend solutions with complex integrations and synchronizations (for example, with the Shopify API).
* **WorkflowHub**: Native iOS client for real-time orchestration, supervision, and monitoring of n8n workflows.
