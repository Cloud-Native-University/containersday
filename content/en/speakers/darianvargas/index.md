Darian Vargas
I was born on October 31, 1990, in the municipality of Sosua, specifically in a neighborhood called La Piedra.

I am the son of a single mother who worked hard to support me, and I was raised by my grandmother, who did not know how to read or write. However, she always told me with great pride that the only thing a poor person must have is dignity and honesty. Additionally, I am an evangelical Christian, specifically Baptist.

I am a telematics engineer by profession. The only thing I want to tell everyone is that I have no qualities of a genius or a magician. The only thing I possess is the passion and desire to break with a system that has not allowed us to move beyond being a developing country. Only education can save us. I do not need to talk much about myself; let the results judge me.
